Meilisearch Cloud charges per project per day, based on the resource tier and region selected for each project.

## How billing works

**Daily billing cycle.** Each project accrues a daily charge based on its resource plan. The charge is prorated if a project is created or deleted mid-day: you only pay for the time the project exists.

**Per-project pricing.** Each project is billed independently. A team with three projects on three different plans is billed the sum of all three project rates.

**No additional usage fees.** Billing is based on the resource tier only. There are no per-query fees, per-document fees, or bandwidth charges.

**Project pausing.** If you need to stop a project temporarily without deleting it and its data, contact support. Paused projects may have reduced or no billing depending on your plan.

## Resource pricing

The price of a project depends on two factors:

1. **Resource tier** (CPU, RAM, and storage allocation)
2. **Region** where the project is hosted

Exact pricing for each tier and region is shown in the Meilisearch Cloud UI when you create or modify a project, and on the [Meilisearch pricing page](https://www.meilisearch.com/pricing).

## Estimating your costs

To estimate your monthly cost:

1. **Determine your index size.** Export or estimate the size of the data you plan to index. A good rule of thumb: the Meilisearch index is typically 2-4x the size of the raw JSON data, depending on the number of indexed attributes.
2. **Choose a tier where RAM comfortably exceeds your index size.** Leave headroom for growth and peak usage.
3. **Multiply the daily rate by 30** for a monthly estimate.

For high-availability or large-scale workloads requiring [sharding and replication](/capabilities/platform/infrastructure/sharding_and_replication), contact [sales@meilisearch.com](mailto:sales@meilisearch.com) for an Enterprise quote.
