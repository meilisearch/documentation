Meilisearch Cloud generates invoices automatically through Stripe. You can view and download them at any time from the Cloud dashboard.

## Viewing invoices

1. Open the [Meilisearch Cloud dashboard](https://cloud.meilisearch.com).
2. Click **Billing** in the left navigation.
3. Select the **Invoices** tab to see your billing history.

## Downloading an invoice

Click the **Download PDF** button next to any invoice in the list. The PDF is generated by Stripe and includes:

- Invoice date and billing period
- Itemized charges per project
- Payment status (paid, pending, failed)
- Your billing address and company name (if configured)

## Invoice details

Invoices reflect all projects active during the billing period. Each line item corresponds to a project and shows the resource tier and number of days billed.

If you need to update the billing name or address shown on invoices, do so via your [payment method settings](/capabilities/platform/billing/payment_methods).
