All payment processing for Meilisearch Cloud is handled by [Stripe](https://stripe.com). You can manage your payment methods from the Billing section of the Cloud dashboard.

## Viewing payment methods

1. Open the [Meilisearch Cloud dashboard](https://cloud.meilisearch.com).
2. Click **Billing** in the left navigation.
3. Select the **Payment methods** tab.

## Adding a payment method

1. Click **Add payment method**.
2. Enter your card details in the Stripe-hosted form.
3. Click **Save**. The new method appears in the list.

If you have multiple payment methods, you can set one as the default. The default method is charged automatically on each billing cycle.

## Updating a payment method

Credit and debit card details cannot be edited in place. To update an expiring card:

1. Add the new card as a new payment method.
2. Set it as the default.
3. Remove the old card if desired.

## Removing a payment method

Click the **Remove** button next to a payment method. You cannot remove the default payment method if there are active projects. Set a different method as default first.

## Payment failures

If a payment fails (for example, due to an expired card), Meilisearch Cloud will notify you by email and retry the charge. Update your payment method promptly to avoid service interruption.
