Search through the complete history of Nobel Prize winners. This demo shows how Meilisearch handles biographical and historical data with rich filtering options.

## Features demonstrated

- **Biographical search**: Find laureates by name or achievement
- **Category filtering**: Filter by prize category (Physics, Chemistry, Peace, etc.)
- **Year filtering**: Search within specific time periods
- **Rich profiles**: Display biographical information and prize details

## Use case

Historical and biographical databases benefit from:

- Full-text search across names and descriptions
- Faceted filtering by category and time period
- Handling of international names and transliterations
- Display of structured biographical data

## Dataset

The Nobel Prize dataset includes:

- All laureates from 1901 to present
- Six categories: Physics, Chemistry, Medicine, Literature, Peace, Economics
- Prize motivation and biographical details
- Shared prizes and organizational recipients

## Technical highlights

| Feature | Implementation |
|---------|----------------|
| Searchable | Name, motivation, affiliation |
| Filterable | Category, year, country |
| Sortable | Year (ascending/descending) |
| Display | Photo, bio, prize details |

## Example searches

- "quantum" - Find physics prizes related to quantum mechanics
- "peace" + filter by year range - Peace prizes from specific decades
- "Marie" - Find Marie Curie's multiple prizes
